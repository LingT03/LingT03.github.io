Undergraduate study at MSU Denver pursued as a **dual-major** track in Computer
Science and Data Science / Machine Learning, with a **mathematics minor** providing
the analytic backbone (real analysis, linear algebra, multivariate calculus,
probability theory).

Core competencies developed during the program:

- **Systems and algorithms**: data structures, complexity analysis, operating
  systems fundamentals, networked computing.
- **Statistical learning**: supervised and unsupervised methods, regularization,
  resampling-based inference, cross-validation.
- **Software engineering**: version control workflows, testing discipline,
  modular Python and R codebases, reproducible analysis pipelines.
- **Applied mathematics**: numerical linear algebra, optimization, probabilistic
  modeling.
